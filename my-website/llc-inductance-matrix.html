About This Calculator

This calculator determines key transformer parameters based on the inductance matrix values. The calculations are based on standard transformer equations that relate the mutual inductance (M), self-inductances (L₁₁ and L₂₂), and turn ratio (N) to the coupling coefficient, leakage inductance, and magnetizing inductance.

When the external choke inductance option is selected, the calculator adds the external inductance value to both the short-circuit and open-circuit primary inductance measurements, simulating the effect of an external inductor in series with the transformer primary winding.
